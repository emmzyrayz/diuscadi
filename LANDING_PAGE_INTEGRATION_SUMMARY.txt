No, it's **not** your Linkserve account password.

The `SMTP_PASS` is the **email account password** — the password you use to **log into the `info@diuscadi.org.ng` email account**.

---

## **How to Find It**

### **Option 1: You Already Created It**
When you created the `info@diuscadi.org.ng` email account in Linkserve control panel, you set a password for it. That's the one you need.

- **Where to check:** Linkserve control panel → Email Accounts → `info@diuscadi.org.ng` → Password (or reset it if you forgot)

### **Option 2: You Don't Remember It**
Go to Linkserve control panel and **reset the email account password**:

1. Log in to **Linkserve control panel**
2. Find **Email Accounts** or **Email Management**
3. Locate `info@diuscadi.org.ng`
4. Click **Reset Password** or **Change Password**
5. Set a new password (use something strong)
6. Use that new password as your `SMTP_PASS`

---

## **Example**

```bash
# Linkserve account password (to log into control panel)
My Account Password: SuperSecret123

# Email account password (for info@diuscadi.org.ng)
SMTP_PASS: EmailPassword456  ← Different! Use this in .env.local
```

---

## **What You Need**

| Item | Where From | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| SMTP_HOST | Linkserve settings | `mail.diuscadi.org.ng` |
| SMTP_PORT | Linkserve settings | `465` |
| SMTP_USER | Your email address | `info@diuscadi.org.ng` |
| **SMTP_PASS** | **Email account password** | `YourEmailPassword123` |
| EMAIL_FROM | Your email address | `info@diuscadi.org.ng` |

So:

```bash
SMTP_HOST=mail.diuscadi.org.ng
SMTP_PORT=465
SMTP_USER=info@diuscadi.org.ng
SMTP_PASS=THE_PASSWORD_FOR_THE_INFO_EMAIL_ACCOUNT  ← This one
EMAIL_FROM=info@diuscadi.org.ng
```

**If you're unsure, reset the password in Linkserve control panel and use the new password.** ✅
